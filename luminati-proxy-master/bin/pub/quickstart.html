Quick start wizard

  • Provide your credentials and click Save settings.
  • Create your first local proxy: go to the Proxies tab, click Add a proxy, then click Save.
  • Modify your proxy's settings: click the pencil icon, increase the port number or modify other properties and hit Save.
    You can also edit settings inline and use action icons to view statistics, history, remove the proxy, etc.
  • Explore Zones and Tools tabs for additional functionality.
